About Us

Our products provide access to a new financial infrastructure that helps anyone in the world to achieve financial freedom.

Since 2014 we are building on Stellar - an open network for storing and moving money. Fiat currencies, stocks, bonds, Bitcoin and other digital currencies are all available on a single network and can be freely exchanged between each other. Imagine a world where payments are fast, cheap and borderless.
We build secure and beautiful products that empower people worldwide to do this.

What we do

Empowering individuals

Whether you’re just getting started with crypto or are an advanced investor, Ultra Stellar gives you the power and confidence to navigate through the new financial world. Our range of digital products helps you invest, securely store and transfer a variety of digital tokens from your desktop or mobile device.

Professional services

Our experience in building blockchain products helps businesses transform their operations for the new financial era. Ultra Stellar is the engineering leader that can help your organization apply the blockchain tech, benefit from the power of decentralized financial networks, integrate with the Stellar network and with our products, and build production-ready solutions.
