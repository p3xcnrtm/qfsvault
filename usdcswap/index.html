Switch to Stellar

Stellar is the best network for moving money. Transactions are confirmed within seconds, and cost far less than a penny (typically you’ll pay 0.00001 XLM). Moving USDC on Ethereum might cost you $20 and takes at least a few minutes.

Swap your USDC 1:1

You’ll get your Stellar USDC once the transfer is confirmed on Ethereum. We take absolutely no fees when you swap Ethereum USDC to Stellar, because fees on Stellar are so small. Anytime you need you can use our tool to swap back to Ethereum paying the current gas fees.

Discover what’s possible

While payments have always been the focus of Stellar, there’s a lot more that you can do. Use StellarX to trade on Stellar DEX, swap USDC to other assets on LOBSTR or even stake your USDC to earn interest in your wallet.

How it works

Step 1

Deposit any amount of Ethereum USDC to Ultra Stellar swap account.

Step 2

Receive the same number of Stellar USDC tokens in return to the address of your choice.

Step 3

Use USDC on Stellar to send payments, trade on SDEX or interact with apps on Stellar.

Step 4

Enjoy the benefits of the Stellar network! Fast transactions, low fees and built-in DEX.
