Why should I buy Litecoin?

Litecoin is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency created by Charlie Lee in 2011. Widely considered to be the first successful “alternative cryptocurrency”, Litecoin was developed to improve on Bitcoin’s shortcomings. It has a faster block generation rate and hence offers a faster transaction confirmation time. Many traders and crypto investors choose Litecoin for its speed and ease of acquisition as its coin limit is 4x higher than that of Bitcoin.
